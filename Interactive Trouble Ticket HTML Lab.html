Interactive Lab: Tablet Running Slow

Scenario: An employee reports their tablet is running slow. As a help desk technician, you must troubleshoot, identify the cause, and document the resolution following CompTIA 220-1202 objectives.

Step 1: Gather Information

The employee says: "My tablet started running slow after yesterday’s update."

Step 2: Establish a Theory

You suspect system services may be impacted by the update.

Step 3: Test the Theory

You confirm the update service is consuming high CPU and memory.

Step 4: Document and Close Ticket

After resolution, you must document the fix.

Sample Help Desk Ticket

Help Desk Ticket

Issue: Tablet running slow after system update.

Steps Taken: Verified symptoms, checked system services, identified update service consuming resources.

Resolution: Restarted tablet, allowed update to complete, optimized startup apps.

Status: Closed – Employee confirmed tablet performance improved.
